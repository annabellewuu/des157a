Game of PIG

Click here for instructions.

Instructions:

Objective:

Be the first player to score 30 points or higher.

Gameplay:

  • Roll the dice to add to your score, avoiding a "one" on either die.
  • If a "one" is rolled, your turn ends, and it's the other player’s turn.
  • After each roll, you can choose to:
    • Roll again to accumulate more points.
    • Pass to the other player if feeling unlucky.

Special Rule:

Rolling two "ones" (snake eyes) zeroes out your current score, so be cautious!

May the dice be in your favor!

Randomly select the first player and begin the game.
